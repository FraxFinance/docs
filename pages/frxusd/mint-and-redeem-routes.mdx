# frxUSD Mint & Redeem Supported Routes & Networks

Mint and redeem frxUSD 1:1 on Ethereum mainnet and cross-chain.

### How to Use

1. **Minting frxUSD**: Deposit the backing asset (USDC, USTB, BUIDL, WTGXX, or USDB) into the corresponding custodian contract to mint frxUSD
2. **Redeeming frxUSD**: Burn frxUSD through the custodian contract to receive the backing asset

## Cross-Chain Mint & Redeem Routes

Use the FraxNet contract system for cross-chain frxUSD mint & redeem operations. This system enables minting and redeeming frxUSD across multiple networks including Fraxtal, Arbitrum, Base, and others using **USDC** as the payment or redemption asset. The same contract powers [FraxNet](https://net.frax.com):

| **Mainnet**  | **FraxNet Factory Address**                                                                                             |
| :----------- | :---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
