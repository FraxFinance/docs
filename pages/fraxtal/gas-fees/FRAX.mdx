# FRAX on Fraxtal

Fraxtal leverages FRAX as the designated gas token, providing users with the capability to utilize FRAX to pay for blockspace.

Users can obtain FRAX within Fraxtal through the native L2 bridge. They can bridge either FRAX (prev. FXS) (an ERC20 token on Ethereum mainnet). Users bridging back FRAX from Fraxtal receive ERC20 FRAX (prev. FXS) on Ethereum mainnet.
